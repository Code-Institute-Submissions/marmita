Who Are We?

From the friendship of a group of young people Marmita was created. A start-up of healthy eating people who believe that it is possible to succeed, doing the right thing the right way!
With the desire to change the world and enjoy the best of life, we live and work intensely to make life easier for our clients, our partners and those around us.
Do we want to embrace the world? Yes, we are moved by great challenges!

Mission

To be a world leader in the promotion of healthy habits for the whole society.

Vision

All people should have access to affordable, healthy food that is fresh, attractive, nutritious, safe and free of contaminants.

Values

Encourage informed choices and demonstrate the impact on personal health and the health of the planet.
